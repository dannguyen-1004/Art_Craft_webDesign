"The trend to use resin in art comes from the USA, Canada and Australia. In Europe, the technology is still little known. With resin you can create works of art that enchant with their clarity, luminosity, brilliance and depth. Color pigments or other additives are added to the resin. Different effects can be created by choosing the painting ground, the type of casting, the colors and the additives. Even experienced resin artists have never finished learning."
